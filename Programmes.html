Master of Business Administration This is a two-year, full-time programme affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University, Kota and approved by AICTE. This programme follows a semester system, six months each semester. The first year consists of core courses. The second year consists of a few core courses and several elective courses. The summer internship is scheduled at the end of the first year. The programme offers dual specialization (major and minor) in the second year. Active learning process is intensive and reinforces individual learning along with class and group activities, using participative and interactive methods. The Methods of learning involve:- Class room Learning, Case Studies, Presentation/Projects/Paper working, Seminars, workshops, Group Discussions, Simulation Management Games, Exposure to industry via guest lectures and industrial internship. For Admission in MBA, the candidate must possess Graduate degree or equivalent from any recognized University with at least 50% marks in Graduation (45% in case of candidates belonging to SC/ST/Non creamy layer OBC/SBC category).
